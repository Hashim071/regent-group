About us

From conception to completion

Property Maintenance Experts Trusted for Over 50 Years
Our story began more than 50 years ago, when Steven Featherstone a skilled tradesman with a strong work ethic founded the business with a clear mission: to provide honest, dependable maintenance services with a personal touch.
For 40 years, we traded as a family-run operation, building lasting relationships and a strong reputation across London and the South East. In 2010, we became a limited company, carrying forward those same values into a new era of growth and development.
